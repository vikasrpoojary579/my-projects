            Kakure College is an educational institute that provides coaching for competitive entrance examinations like Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) and National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET). 
            The main aim of the college is to help students achieve their dream of becoming engineers and doctors by providing high-quality academic coaching.

Kakure College focuses on building a strong foundation in science subjects such as Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and Biology. 
These subjects are essential for success in national-level entrance examinations.
    
The Joint Entrance Examination – Advanced is one of the most competitive engineering entrance exams in India and is used for admission into prestigious institutes like the IITs.

            
                

                    The mission of Kakure College is to:
                    
    


  • Provide quality coaching for JEE and NEET aspirants
    • 


  • Help students develop strong conceptual understanding
    • 


  • Support students with regular tests, practice sessions, and doubt-clearing classes
    • 


  • Guide students to achieve top ranks in competitive exams
